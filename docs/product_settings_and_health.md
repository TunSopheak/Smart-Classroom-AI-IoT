# Phase 11 Product Settings and System Health

## Goal

Make the Smart Classroom system feel more like a real product, not only a demo.

## Added Pages

/dashboard/product-settings
/dashboard/system-health
/api/system-health

## Product Settings

The teacher/admin can configure product name, institution name, timezone, camera index, recording format, auto behavior default, behavior thresholds, and privacy note.

## System Health

The system checks database connection, OpenCV availability, recording storage, camera service, important product pages, and product readiness checklist.
