# Phase 14 Product AI and Attendance Integration Center

## Goal

Make important AI and attendance features visible and teacher-friendly.

## Added Pages

```text
/dashboard/product-center
/dashboard/qr-attendance
/dashboard/face-training
/dashboard/monitoring-workspace
/api/face-recognition-live/stream
```

## Main Improvements

- Product Feature Center
- QR Attendance Center
- Face Training Center
- Upload face photos
- Capture face samples from camera
- Train LBPH model from dashboard
- Live face recognition stream with student ID, name, and confidence
- Phone detection readiness panel

## Phone Detection Note

Current version supports manual phone usage behavior events and overlays. Full automatic phone detection should be integrated later with YOLO/object detection.
